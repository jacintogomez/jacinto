Virtual Reality Hexapod

This is a virtual reality simulator that mimics the acceleration of gravity as well as movement visuals. The mouse is stationary on a spinning head bar and is on top of an omni-directional treadmill that records its rotations and movements. The screens respond with a visual replica of the appropriate movements and the entire apparatus is on a hexapod that simulates the acceleration of gravity for everything to feel more realistic. The goal of the mouse is to find a target projected on screen and navigate to it, at which point it will receive a juice reward from the spout in front of it.

Virtual Reality Training Rig

This is designed to be a miniature version of the previous hexapod setup, for the purpose of training mice to use the system before being put into the larger and more complicated one. The system here was built to train the mouse to only move forward/backward, and become accustomed to receiving an award for hitting its movement target. It uses a custom designed mouse head holder that can be adjusted for the specific mouse height and body length.
